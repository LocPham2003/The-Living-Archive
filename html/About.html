About the Living Archives

How did it get started ?

On a summer evening, I went to a Pho restaurant near my home. Overwhelmed by its vintage and vibrant design, I had come to realize how amazing and nostalgic the life of Vietnam in the twentieth century was. With my burning passion for History, I created The Living Archives to portray the History of Vietnam, in the hope to enlighten the modern generation of the beauty of the life in Vietnam in the past. Hopefully, my posts here will give you the same vibe as I did back at that Pho restaurant :D.
